  • Analyze the pattern to determine what comes next
  • Difficulty increases with more complex patterns

Grid Puzzle

A Lights Out-style puzzle where clicking a tile changes its state and the state of adjacent tiles.

  • Turn all lights on (or off) to win
  • Plan your moves carefully - each click affects multiple tiles
  • Difficulty increases with larger grids and different patterns
  • Challenges spatial reasoning and strategic thinking

Logic Gates Challenge

Arrange logic gates (AND, OR, NOT, etc.) to achieve specified outputs from given inputs.

  • Drag and connect logic gates to create circuits
  • Learn the basics of computational thinking
  • Test your solutions against multiple input combinations
  • Perfect introduction to digital logic principles
